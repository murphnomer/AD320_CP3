More information on the Stroop effect, on which this project is based: Stroop Effect

You can use the None language mode on the left to "baseline" your performance with colored boxes instead of words. Compare your response time and mismatch numbers to this baseline to get a rough idea of how well you understand the text in the gameboard area. The better you understand the colored words at a glance, the higher your mismatch and response time numbers should be as the interference effect becomes more pronounced.

If you have a very high Wrong number, it's likely that you don't understand the language on the response buttons very well.

Play around with it and see if you can gain some more insights!
